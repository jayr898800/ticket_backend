How to Use Your Ticket

  1. After submitting your repair request, a unique Ticket Number will be generated for you.
  2. Copy and save this Ticket Number. You’ll need it to check the status of your unit.
  3. Visit our website: www.ronaldselectronicshop.com
  4. Go to the Check Ticket Status page.
  5. Enter your Ticket Number to view real-time updates: Pending, Ongoing, or Completed.
  6. Always keep your Ticket Number safe; it is your proof of request and your key to updates.
